Our Achievements

Honored for our commitment to creating a positive social impact.

Swachh Survekshan 2024 - Rank 3

Lucknow secured 3rd position among Indian cities with over 10 lakh population. Ekatva played a key role by conducting targeted social media campaigns and organizing citizen engagement activities.

Water+ City Certification

Lucknow was declared a Water+ Certified City, recognizing its efforts in wastewater management and sustainable sanitation, supported by Ekatva's digital campaigns.

Boosting Civic Participation

With Ekatva as the digital arm, Lucknow Nagar Nigam witnessed record engagement, turning civic updates into participatory conversations and increasing citizen feedback.

Excellence in Waste Management

Supported door-to-door garbage collection awareness and nala safai (drain cleaning) drives with compelling before-after visuals and educational posts.

Plastic-Free & Clean-Green Campaigns

Boosted public participation in initiatives like Plastic-Free Lucknow and tree plantation drives through engaging content, contests, and influencer reels.

Smart City Digital Coverage

Ensured high visibility for Smart City developments, from LED street lighting to digital waste tracking, through appealing visuals and regular updates.
